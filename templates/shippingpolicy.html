Shipping Policy

At ShopSmart, we are committed to delivering your orders in a timely manner. Below are the details of our shipping policy:

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and the shipping method selected. You can view the shipping costs at checkout.

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped.

International Shipping

We offer international shipping to select countries. Additional fees may apply for customs and duties.
