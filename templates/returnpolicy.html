Return Policy

At ShopSmart, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you can return your item under the following conditions:

Return Period

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be unused and in the original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns.
  • Certain items, such as personalized or custom products, are not eligible for return.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at support@shopsmart.com. We will provide you with a return shipping label and instructions.

Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after we receive your returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.
